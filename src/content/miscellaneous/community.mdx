# Community

This section highlights useful learning resources and tools built and maintained by the Epic Stack community. You'll find information about libraries, examples, articles, and videos that can enhance your understanding and use of the Epic Stack.

## Learning Resources

While the primary learning resources for the Epic Stack are official platforms, the community has also contributed valuable content to support developers.

## Contributing to Community Resources

We encourage the community to contribute their knowledge and experiences with the Epic Stack. If you've created a resource that could benefit others, consider sharing it:

1. Create a pull request to add your resource to this page.
2. Ensure your contribution is relevant and provides value to Epic Stack users.
3. Include a brief description of your resource and how it relates to the Epic Stack.

## Community Support

For questions, discussions, and support, you can:

1. Join the <ExternalLink url='https://github.com/epicweb-dev/epic-stack/discussions'>Epic Stack Discussions on GitHub</ExternalLink>
2. Follow <ExternalLink url='https://github.com/epicweb-dev/epic-stack/discussions'>@epicwebdev</ExternalLink> on Twitter for updates and tips

By leveraging these community resources and contributing your own, you can enhance your Epic Stack development experience and help others in the community grow as well.
